Mistakes

Common Small Business Tax Mistakes to Avoid

Most mistakes are recordkeeping problems rather than complicated tax planning issues. Better systems make BAS and tax return work easier.

Mixing Personal and Business Costs

Private spending should be separated from business records so deductions are clearer and easier to support.

Missing Source Documents

Bank transactions alone may not explain what a purchase was for. Invoices, receipts and notes can matter.

Leaving Bookkeeping Until Year End

Late bookkeeping can make BAS, payroll and tax return work slower and more stressful.
