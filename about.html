Hi, I'm Lawrence.

I founded YSE Finance because I saw a real gap in the market — people needed a tax agent who was not just technically excellent, but also genuinely easy to talk to. Too often, clients feel intimidated or confused by their accountant. I wanted to change that. 我创立YSE Finance，是因为我看到市场上有一个真实的缺口——人们需要的不仅是技术过硬的税务代理，更需要一个真正好沟通的人。太多客户在面对会计师时感到紧张或困惑。我想改变这一点。

Tax doesn't have to be stressful. My job is to make it simple, transparent, and even a little reassuring.

With a background in accounting and years of experience working with Sydney individuals and small businesses, I bring both technical depth and practical know-how to every engagement. 凭借会计专业背景和多年服务悉尼个人及小企业的经验，我将深厚的专业知识和实际操作经验融入每一次服务。无论是简单的个人报税，还是复杂的投资结构，我都同样认真对待每一位客户。

Being bilingual in English and Mandarin means I can serve both the local Australian community and the Chinese-speaking community in Sydney — without anyone feeling lost in translation. 中英双语的能力让我能够同时服务本地澳洲客户和悉尼华人社区——不再有语言障碍，沟通更顺畅，理解更到位。

Outside of work, I'm passionate about financial education and helping people understand their rights and obligations as taxpayers. 工作之外，我热衷于财务教育，帮助大家了解自己作为纳税人的权利和义务。了解越多，决策越好——而我就在这里帮助你。
