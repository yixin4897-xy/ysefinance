Documents

Documents You May Need

The exact checklist depends on your income sources and records. YSE Finance can confirm what is relevant after an initial conversation.

Income records: income statements, bank interest, dividends, managed fund statements, business or side income records.
Deduction support: receipts, invoices, logbooks, diary records, work-related expense evidence and professional fee invoices.
Property and investment records: rental statements, loan interest, property expenses, broker reports, purchase and sale documents.
Crypto and foreign income records: exchange reports, wallet summaries, overseas bank statements, foreign tax documents and currency conversion notes if available.
Records

Work-Related Deductions and Record Keeping

Work-related deductions generally need a clear connection to earning your income and appropriate records. YSE Finance can help review whether your documents are organised enough for lodgement.
